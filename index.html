Biography

I am William Holmes, I am a dual degree, dual minor graduate from the university of New Mexico. My degrees are a BS in Anthropology & a BS in Geography. My minors are in history and management. I have extensive knowledge on computers, surveying, and GIS work, these are my main focus in my studies. I hope to use my skills to manage a team of archaeologist and GIS analysts in order to make more discoveries in the field of archaeology. I want to become a pilot and a drone pilot and use my skills to help my team in the field. I have 1 paper published and will be giving a presentation on it, to the SAA later in 2025
